Lesson Plan

This will be the document where I implement the lessons I learn through the Odin Project.

The goal for the webpage is a simple D20 roller. It will have a background of a forest with an evil knight and a wizard to the left and right, respectively. When it rolls a nat 1, the knight will change from idle to a slash image and cut the D20 die. When it rolls a nat 20, the wizard will change from idle to a casting image and ignite the D20 die.

For now, I want the die to have a slot machine type of animation.
